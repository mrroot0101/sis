About AquaFlow

We're on a mission to provide sustainable water solutions to Ukrainian farmers and communities facing energy challenges

Resilient Water Access for a Resilient Ukraine

AquaFlow Solar Solutions addresses the critical challenge of disrupted water access in Ukraine by providing and installing decentralized solar-powered water pumping systems. Leveraging Ukraine's abundant solar resource and the urgent need for resilient infrastructure, we offer easy-to-deploy, robust, and low-maintenance solutions for borewells, rivers, and groundwater sources.

Our mission is to empower Ukrainian communities and farmers with reliable water supply, fostering food security, agricultural resilience, and improved public health amidst ongoing instability and future reconstruction efforts.

The protracted conflict in Ukraine has inflicted severe damage upon critical civilian infrastructure, none more vital and vulnerable than the national energy grid. Relentless attacks on power generation facilities and transmission networks have resulted in widespread and frequent blackouts, plunging entire regions into darkness and disrupting essential services.
